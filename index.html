Spanish Community Center (current) Home Intervention Program - H.I.P AHEC

Two Service Locations One Mission

The Spanish Community Center (SCC) is a 501 (c)3 Minority Community Based Organization that has been providing Social Service programs to the community since 1974. Spanish Community Center provides Bi-lingual and Culturally competent services. SCC programs promote self-sufficiency and support.
