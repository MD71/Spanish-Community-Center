Two Service Locations

One Mission

The Spanish Community Center (SCC) is a 501 (c)3 Minority Community Based Organization that has been providing Social Service programs to the community since 1974. Spanish Community Center provides Bi-lingual and Culturally competent services. SCC programs promote self-sufficiency and support.

Home Intervention Program (H.I.P)

The Home Intervention Program provides Bi-Lingual/Bi-Cultural supportive services to families who have special qualifying risk factors. H.I.P. provides a combination of in-home social and supportive services coupled with parenting education to identified families involved with the Child Protective Services-DCP&P. This program assists parents in giving them skills they need to develop cooperation, responsibility and good character in their children. Learn More!

Shore Area Health Education Center (AHEC)

The National AHEC Organization supports and advances the Area Health Education Center (AHEC) Network to improve health by leading the nation in recruitment, training and retention of a diverse health work force for underserved communities.
