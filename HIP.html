Active Parenting

Active Parenting curriculum is an Adlerian evidence-based parent education program. This program examines specific parenting profiles and using choice in reducing power struggles and in gaining cooperation; the “think-feel-do” cycle in guiding a child’s decision making process; and how to utilize the “BANK” method of encouragement in imparting values and building strength.
